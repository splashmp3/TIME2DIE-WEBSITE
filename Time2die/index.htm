Server Overview

Welcome to Time2Die, an immersive and adrenaline-pumping full PvP DayZ server crafted to deliver an unmatched gaming experience. Whether you're a battle-hardened survivor or just venturing into the DayZ world, our server offers a dynamic and engaging environment where every choice has consequences and each encounter could be your last.

Custom Keycard Systems

Dive into our innovative DNA keycard system, where keycards are strategically placed in custom locations throughout the map. This system introduces an element of exploration and strategy, requiring players to uncover hidden areas and gain access to valuable resources by locating and using these keycards. The custom placement adds layers of challenge and discovery to your survival journey.

Trader

Our server features a bespoke trader with a meticulously balanced economy. Unlike standard traders, our custom trader offers a carefully curated selection of items at fair and strategic prices. This system ensures a meaningful trading experience where players must think critically about their purchases and trades to enhance their survival chances.

Black Markets

Engage with our exclusive black market, a high-risk, high-reward trading hub situated outside conventional safe zones. This custom black market introduces an element of danger and excitement, challenging players to navigate through perilous conditions to access rare and valuable goods. The absence of a safety net makes every transaction a thrilling experience.

Unique Challenges and Events

Stay on your toes with our diverse range of events, including daily airdrops and King of the Hills (KOTH) challenges that add competitive and cooperative dynamics to the gameplay. Additionally, we host special community events designed to foster interaction and competition, keeping the gaming experience fresh and exhilarating.
